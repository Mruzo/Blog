About me

Hello there, I'm Christopher Uzoewulu, I’m a Web Development enthusiast from Nigeria, currently living in Canada. I write about my thoughts on how we could be motivated to live better and achieve greater feats as a society.

My professional background is pretty diverse; I’m a Civil Engineering graduate, Certified Associate in Project Management, Certified Quality Technician, and I’m currently working as a Data Quality Administrator. Because I love to learn new things, my career interest has taken a few turns over the past 5years. In that time, I’ve switched between seven jobs(part time and full time), and met a tonne of wonderful people that have helped me make some positive life changing decisions.

I built this web app primarily as a side project to improve my Django skills, and for the most part, I had no idea what kind of content it was going to have. I took a short trip into my past and figured I’ve indirectly learned more about life from literally over a thousand people, so I thought this would be a good platform to share my learning with you.

The name of this blog(in as much as I don’t like to call it this), was self inspired. I figured most people(me) don’t always want to listen to motivational speakers talk about how/why they should “aspire to acquire the desire that they admire”, but deep down, they need that extra push - that inner energy to get through a project or a difficult time or to recognize and appreciate their little gains. From experience, there’s no better feeling than when that motivation nicely sneaks up on you.

I hope you enjoy my articles well enough to spread the word, and please add your two cents and reactions, because I'd love to learn from you too.

Feel free to contact me for jobs, and you can also check me out on LinkedIn.
